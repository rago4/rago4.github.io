📚 Books

Here you can find a list of the books that I have recently read. Each book is rated in a 5-star scale and summarized in short sentence.

The Last Wish

by Andrzej Sapkowski
⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️

The first part of the story of Geralt of Rivia, full of sarcastic humor and impressive stories, yet a bit hard to read cause of old-fashioned language and various characters.

(5 June, 2020)

Pet Sematary

by Stephen King
⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️

Blood chilling Creeds' family story that sometimes was just too much to handle, but at the same time it was so addictive I couldn't stop reading.

(May 20, 2020)

The Sicilian (1984)

by Mario Puzo
⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️ ⭐️

A long, sometimes boring but definitely beautiful story about the adventures of a brave young man named Salvatore and about his beautiful and at the same time brutal home - Sicily.

(May 06, 2020)
